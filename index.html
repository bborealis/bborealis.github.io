About

I am currently a student at Epicodus learning PHP, Javascript, AngularJS, jQuery, HTML, CSS, pair-programming, and Drupal.

My previous experience is in the specialty coffee industry where I was the General Manger of a busy cafe.

My desire for personal growth and my inquisitive nature brought me to the world of web development.

I am excited to become a developer because I enjoy the never ending process of learning and I love discovering solutions to problems.

Circles and Squares of Doom

This is a game built with AngularJS in the style of Two Dots and Bejewled.

This was an Epicodus group project I did with Kevin Tokheim, Ben Spenard, Jason Bethel, & Steve Smietana. It was a great oppurtunity to work in a large group and practice commiting to different branches using Git.

What I was most pleased with in this project was the work I did to get the flames to move down the board as the game progressed.
